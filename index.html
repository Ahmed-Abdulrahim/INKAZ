How to avoid highway accidents

We'd be happy to share some advice with you regarding how to prevent mishaps.

Use your signals

Use your turn signals to indicate your intentions when changing lanes or turning. This will help other drivers anticipate your movements and avoid collisions

Avoid aggressive driving

Aggressive driving, such as tailgating, speeding, and weaving in and out of traffic, can increase your risk of a collision. Drive defensively and be patient

Keep your vehicle maintained

Properly maintaining your vehicle can reduce your risk of an accident. Ensure that your brakes, tires, and lights are in good working condition

Be aware of weather conditions

Inclement weather, such as rain, snow, or fog, can make driving on the highway more challenging. Slow down and use caution when driving in these conditions

Follow traffic rules

Traffic rules are designed to keep drivers and passengers safe on the road. Follow posted speed limits, traffic signals, and signs, and always wear a seatbelt

Stay alert

Avoid distractions while driving, such as using your phone or eating while behind the wheel. Stay focused on the road and be aware of your surroundings
